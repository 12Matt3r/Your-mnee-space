## 2024-05-22 - MNEE Dark Theme Focus States
**Learning:** The application uses a custom "MNEE" dark theme (`bg-mnee-charcoal`). Standard focus rings are often invisible or low contrast.
**Action:** For interactive elements on dark backgrounds, use: `focus:outline-none focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-mnee-gold focus-visible:ring-offset-2 focus-visible:ring-offset-mnee-charcoal`.
